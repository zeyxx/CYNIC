## 2. Idea

**What is the problem your company is trying to solve? How are you solving it? (600 characters max):**
Chess products capture performance (win/loss) but don't help players understand *how* they play (risk, hesitation, patience). Simultaneously, Web3 identity relies on farmable OAuth or intrusive biometrics. We solve both. Blitz & Chill offers a low-pressure chess experience where meaningful choices become behavioral signals, providing an evolving mirror of personal progression. Long-term, these complex, unforgeable decision patterns form Talaria: a portable, optional onchain reputation and Proof of Humanity oracle for the Solana ecosystem.

**What drove you to this idea? How do you know that there is a burning need for what you are building? (600 characters max):**
The chess market is polarized: competitive products create ladder anxiety, leaving a gap for a relaxing, guest-first experience that rewards self-discovery. In parallel, emerging Web3 governance (like futarchy) desperately needs sybil resistance weighted by real human behavior, not just capital or KYC. Chess gameplay offers complex decision-making that is trivial to verify but impossible for simple bots to mimic. By making chess pleasurable again, we organically generate the behavioral signals needed for a sovereign Web3 identity layer.

**Describe your founder-market fit. What makes you the right team to build this? (600 characters max):**
We bridge game theory, autonomous organisms, and decentralized identity. T. architects the CYNIC engine, the sovereign "phantom" backend designed to ingest high-frequency data and translate it into cryptographic evidence. S. drives the Blitz & Chill product vision, creating an engaging chess experience that users actually want to play. Together, we execute a dual strategy: CYNIC runs invisibly to ensure sovereignty, while Blitz & Chill acts as our ultimate moat—generating an exclusive stream of human behavioral data that no competitor can easily replicate.

**How does your product / idea use Solana? (600 characters max):**
Solana plays a central role in our architecture due to its unmatched capability to handle high-frequency state. Every game on Blitz & Chill generates a massive stream of temporal and behavioral patterns. Our CYNIC backend ingests this data, but to make this identity truly sovereign and tamper-proof, we rely on Solana. Its ultra-low latency and micro-transaction costs are uniquely capable of sealing cryptographic evidence of play in real-time, allowing us to offer optional, unforgeable wallet-bound reputation to protocols that need our Proof of Humanity.

**Who is your competition, and what do you understand about your business that they don’t? (600 characters max):**
Traditional platforms focus on rank. Web3 identity projects measure "is this a unique meatbag?" or "can this script click OAuth?". Identity is an evolving lived path. AI can mimic chess moves, but perfectly faking unique temporal signatures, macro-behavioral shifts, and learning patterns across hundreds of sessions is practically impossible. Our PoH makes sybil-farming computationally and economically prohibitive. By turning play into a behavioral mirror, we create a high-cost barrier against bots without relying on intrusive biometrics.

**What’s the path to this business generating $10M in annual revenue? What needs to be true? (600 characters max):**
We monetize through optional cosmetics, advanced coaching features on Blitz & Chill, and API/transaction fees for our reputation oracle. To reach $10M, we need B&C to capture a fraction of the casual and Web3 gaming market. Once our player base creates a critical mass of behavioral data, our PoH oracle becomes highly valuable to top-tier DAOs and DeFi protocols requiring sybil resistance. As the standard for identity shifts towards behavioral verification, Talaria becomes a foundational identity layer.

**Can your product be used on mobile devices?**
Yes, Blitz & Chill and the Talaria frontend are web-based and highly mobile-responsive.
