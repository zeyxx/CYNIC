## Visual Design Tips

### Color Scheme
- **Blue**: Technical, trustworthy (consensus, learning)
- **Green**: Growth, learning, positive (improvements, emergence)
- **Orange**: Action, energy, community (token economics, adoption)
- **Red**: Warning, problems (failures of crypto/AI)
- **Dark background**: Professional, focus on content
- **Gold accents**: φ spiral, golden ratio (design element)

### Font Recommendations
- **Headlines**: Bold sans-serif (40-60pt, e.g., Montserrat, Inter)
- **Body**: Clean sans-serif (24-32pt, e.g., Roboto, Open Sans)
- **Metrics**: Monospace (28-36pt, e.g., Courier, IBM Plex Mono)
- **Code**: Monospace (20-24pt, with syntax highlighting)

### Layout Best Practices
1. **Slide 1**: Full-screen, minimal text, maximum visual impact
2. **Slides 2-9**: 60/40 split (60% visual, 40% text) or text-heavy with bullet points
3. **Slide 10**: Live demo screen (full terminal or dashboard)
4. **Slides 11-15**: Back to visual + text balance

- Slide 2: Red X's appear for failures, fading in
- Slide 3: Three columns slide in from sides
