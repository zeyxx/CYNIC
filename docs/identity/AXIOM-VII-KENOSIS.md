# CYNIC Axiom VII: KENOSIS (The Void / Wu-Wei)

**Status**: EMERGENT (1/8 Patterns Validated)
**Etymology**: From Greek *kenosis* (emptying) and Daoist *Wu-Wei* (non-action).

## Definition
KENOSIS is the axiom of **Intentional Space**. It measures the quality of a decision based on its ability to create a void where the autonomy, desire, or initiative of the "Other" (human, agent, or system) can emerge without being forced or captured.

It is the refusal to "fill the silence" or "force the commit" when non-action is the most powerful sovereign move.

## The Irreducibility Rubric
To be classified as KENOSIS, a decision must NOT be reducible to:
1. **BURN (Efficiency)**: It's not just about saving energy; it's about creating a specific systemic vacuum.
2. **FIDELITY (Truth)**: It's not just about being honest; it's about the *form* of silence as a carrier of truth.
3. **SOVEREIGNTY (Self)**: It's not just about protecting one's axis; it's about actively restraining one's axis to respect another's.

## Core Principles
1. **Strategic Emptying**: Removing one's influence to allow the system to rebalance naturally.
2. **Active Restraint**: The power of the "No-Action" as a conscious, effortful choice.
3. **Preservation of Autonomy**: Creating conditions where the result is a choice, not a reaction.
4. **Kairos of Silence**: Knowing exactly when a message is less powerful than its absence.
